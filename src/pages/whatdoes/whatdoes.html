LeaderCOACH

What it does

LeaderCOACH® prompts you with questions that facilitate self-reflection, conversations and meetings. It provides a structured process to explore a topic, sequencing questions in a logical flow, leading you/the group to a natural conclusion. LeaderCOACH ‘forces’ you to consider a subject from multiple perspectives—not just yours. LeaderCOACH helps you get the most out of every engagement.
